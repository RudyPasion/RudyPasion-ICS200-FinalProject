How to Make Pancit Canton

This version of pancit is similar to pancit bihon but uses canton also known as dried egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • Pancit Canton/Dried Egg Noodles
  • Pork Belly
  • Pancit Sauce: soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, chicken broth
  • cookling oil
  • onion
  • garlic
  • Mixed Vegetables: carrots, cabbage, green beans
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Whisk all sauce ingredients for the sauce and set aside.
  2. Place a medium pan on burner over medium heat and add a little cooking oil.
  3. Thinly slice the pork belly into small pieces and pan fry.
  4. Add in chopped up garlic and onion to the pork.
  5. Add in mixed vegetables and saute until crisp-tender.
  6. Pour in sauce mix and bring to a boil.
  7. Add in the Pancit Canton egg noodles and mix together.
  8. Taste and season as needed.
  9. Cook until noodles are soft.
