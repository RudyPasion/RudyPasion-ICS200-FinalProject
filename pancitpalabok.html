How to Make Pancit Palabok

This version of pancit is similar to pancit malabon but uses thinner noodles and different toppings.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil
  • Ground Pork
  • Anatto Powder
  • Pork Broth
  • Shrimp Bouillon
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thin Rice Noodles
  • Toppings: firm tofu, tinapa flakes, hard boiled eggs, cooked shrimp, green onion

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in water for 15 minutes. Then drain and set aside.
  2. In a sauce pan, add oil and bring to medium heat.
  3. Put in ground pork and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, dilute annato powder in pork broth and pour into saucepan.
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Add shrimp cubes and simmer for 3 minutes.
  7. Add flour gradually while stirring.
  8. Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  9. Simmer until sauce becomes thick and set aside.
  10. In a pot, boil water and cook noodles. Then drain and place on serving tray.
  11. Pour sauce on top of the noodles.
  12. Add toppings to decorate the dish.
