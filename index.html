Four Ways to Make Pancit

Pancit Bihon

Pancit Bihon is the most common type of pancit which is made with bihon noodles (rice vermicelli), a thin rice noodle. It is commonly made with chicken or pork, as well as a mix of vegetables, such as cabbage, carrots, and green beans.

Pancit Canton

Pancit Canton is very similar to Pancit Bihon but uses a thicker noodle known as egg noodles. It can also be customizable being adding your choice of chicken or pork, as well as a mix of vegetables that consists of snap peas, cabbage, carrots, and green beans.

Pancit Malabon

Pancit Malabon originates from Malabon, Manila. This version of pancit shines the spotlight on seafood as you have the option to add shrimp, squid, or mussels. The sauce of this pancit is another factor that makes it unique from the others.

Pancit Palabok

Pancit Palabok utilizes the same noodles as Pancit Bihon. It has a very delicious shrimp infused sauce, and is topped with shrimp, boiled eggs, green onion, and crushed chicharon.
