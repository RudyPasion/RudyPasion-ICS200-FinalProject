How to Make Pancit Bihon

This version of pancit consists of bihon noodles which are known as thin rice vermicelli.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Oil
  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Bihon Noodles
  • Mixed Vegetables: green beans, carrots, and cabbage
  • Pancit Sauce: sodium chicken stock, dark soy sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar.
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Whisk all sauce ingredients for the sauce and set aside.
  2. Place a medium pan on burner over medium heat and add a little cooking oil.
  3. Pan fry the chicken until they are cooked through, then set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add chopped garlic and onion, and cook until fragnent.
  5. Add chicken pieces back in and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add the vegetables and stir fry until soft but still crunchy.
  7. Add and mix in the sauce and bring the pan to a boil
  8. Add dry pancit bihon noodles.
  9. Mix them in with the rest of the ingredients.
  10. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  11. Add chicken stock as needed to have it cook through.
