How to Make Pancit Malabon

This version of pancit shines the spotlight on seafood.

Ingredients

  • Thick Rice Noodles
  • Cooking Oil
  • Achuete Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Knorr Shimp Cube
  • Knorr Pork Cube
  • Sliced Pork Belly
  • Shelled and Deveined Shrimp
  • Slice Squid
  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Topping: sping onion, crushed chicharon, tinapa flakes

Instructions

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package and set aside.
  2. In a pan, add cooking oil and turn to medium heat.
  3. Saute garlic until brown.
  4. Reduce heat and add flour and mix until you get a thick paste.
  5. Add water and bring to a boil. Then add knorr pork and shrimp cubes.
  6. Stirr until dissolved.
  7. Add fish sauce to taste
  8. Simmer for 6 to 8 minutes. Then add shrimp, squid, and pork belly.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Simmer until cooked.
  11. Place noodles into serving dish and pour sauce over it.
  12. Top dish with toppings to decorate.
